About us

Bangladesh Olympiadians Club is a non-profit organization which works for spreading Olympiads to everywhere in Bangladesh. Our primary target is to inform every Olympiad news to Olympiadians and make them more efficient to these Olympiads. Already we organized 4 Big Events, 2 Workshops, 2 Live "Olympiad Adda" Session, 6 Tiny Quiz Contests and we're continuing our regular activities such as Weekly Subject-Wise Problem Solving, Problem Solving on Website, Weekly Quiz Contest etc.

Our Works

We're committed to spread Olympiads all over the Bangladesh

We provide all Olympiad news

Olympiadians often misses Olympiads for not knowing about when will the Olympiad happen. Stay with BOC and you'll never miss any national Olympiad.

We organize various online events

Olympiadians registered for a Olympiad but they often don't know how to do better, what's the syllabus in the Olympiad etc. We organize various online events for practice of upcoming Olympiads.

We organize online workshops

Olympiad isn't limited to textbook. To understand the question pattern and boost Olympiadians' skill on those creative questions, we organize workshops to train our Olympiadians
