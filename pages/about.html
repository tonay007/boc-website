About us

Bangladesh Olympiadians Club is a non-profit organization which works for spreading Olympiads to everywhere in Bangladesh. Our primary target is to inform every Olympiad news to Olympiadians and make them more efficient to these Olympiads. Already we organized 4 Big Events, 2 Workshops, 2 Live "Olympiad Adda" Session, 6 Tiny Quiz Contests and we're continuing our regular activities such as Weekly Subject-Wise Problem Solving, Problem Solving on Website, Weekly Quiz Contest etc.

Olympiadians' Facilities

  • Olympiad News
  • Problem Solving
  • Participation

Olympiadians will get updates about Olympiad news by our Website, Facebook Page and other social medias

Email Updates

Olympiadians will get updates through email about Olympiads, BOC Events, BOC Workshops and others

Website Notification

In the 'Notification' option inside 'My Account', you can also see notification about all blog posts, including Olympiads, Events and so on.

Olympiadians will get opportunity of participating in Problem Solving on our website

Problem posting and answering

Olympiadians can post problems in our 'Problem Solving' option in our website. Also they can solve other Olympiadians' problems.

16/7 Support if you got stuck

Premium Olympiadians (Upcoming Membership Package) will get 16/7 supports from BOC Academic Department if they got stuck in any Problems.

Olympiadians can participate in event and workshops organized by Bangladesh Olympiadians Club. Registration fee is not required for Premium Olympiadians

Events

We organize big online events for boosting Olympiadians' skills on specific topic/subject. Olympiadians can participate in these events and win attractive prizes!

Workshops

We organize workshops for boosting Olympiadians' skills on specific topic/subject. These workshops' classes are taken by skilled trainers. Olympiadians can participate in these workshops and boost their success rate at Olympiads!
